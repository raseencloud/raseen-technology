# Purpose

This document establishes the legal, governance, ethical, and compliance foundation of Raseen Technology before commencing any commercial or financial activities.

The company is committed to building a sustainable and fully compliant business that adheres to applicable laws, regulations, professional standards, and ethical principles.

# Vision

Raseen Technology aims to become a trusted technology company by combining innovation with governance, compliance, professional ethics, and long-term sustainability.

# Core Principle

> **Compliance Before Revenue**

Raseen Technology shall not receive payments, subscriptions, investments, donations, or conduct commercial transactions until the applicable legal, regulatory, governance, taxation, banking, and financial requirements have been properly researched, documented, and implemented.

Trust is built before profit.
